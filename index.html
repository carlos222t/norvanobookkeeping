About Norvana

We started Norvana because founders deserve a bookkeeper who feels like part of the team.

Too many small businesses get treated like a ticket number by big accounting firms — or worse, left guessing with a spreadsheet nobody's touched in months. Norvana was built to be different: a boutique bookkeeping partner that combines meticulous, GAAP-grade accuracy with the kind of relationship where you can actually call someone and get a straight answer.

Every client is paired with a dedicated bookkeeper who learns your business, not just your ledger. We handle the numbers so you can focus on the work only you can do.
