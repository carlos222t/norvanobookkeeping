How it works

From a question to a real answer, in four steps.

No new accounts to remember, no dashboards to decode — just the questions parents already ask, answered the moment they think of them.

01

Log in

Parents log in through the existing school Parent Portal. The AI already knows which student is theirs — nothing new to set up.

02

Ask naturally

"How is Sofia doing in math?" Ask by voice or text, the same way you'd text a friend.

03

AI retrieves the data

Grades, MAP scores, attendance, teacher comments — pulled straight from approved school systems. Never invented, never guessed.

04

Get a clear answer

A parent-friendly explanation of what the data means, plus one thing worth trying at home this week.

Real data, always

The AI never invents an answer

Every response is pulled straight from your school's own approved systems — nothing made up, nothing guessed.

Grades Teacher comments MAP Growth scores Reading levels (Lexile) Attendance Behavior records Approaches to Learning Homework completion Project portfolios Assessment results Counselor notes Service learning Athletics Clubs & activities

Private by design

Parents only ever see information for their own children.

FERPA-aligned

Built to follow FERPA and applicable Mexican privacy regulations, with every interaction logged for accountability.

Never trains on your data

Your school's data stays yours. Conversations are encrypted end to end and never used to train the model.

Who it's for

Built for the whole school community

Not a replacement for teachers — a way to make every conversation more accessible, informed, and efficient.

For parents

Answers, anytime

  • 24/7 access to student information
  • Clear explanations without educational jargon
  • Consistent communication across every teacher
  • Personalized suggestions for supporting learning at home
  • More productive parent-teacher conferences
For teachers

Conference time, reclaimed

  • Less time explaining information parents could review beforehand
  • An AI-generated briefing before every conference
  • More time for goal-setting, support, and student wellbeing
  • Talking points based on questions parents submitted in advance
For students

Growth, not just grades

  • Parents become better informed and more engaged
  • Progress highlighted over time, not just a single score
  • Space to reflect: "What should I improve before next term?"
